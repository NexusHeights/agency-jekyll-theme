Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 26, 2019

Nexus Heights, LLC publishes the Grocery Master app available on the iOS App Store.

This page lists our privacy policy as it pertains to Grocery Master, and how we manage and use your personal data.

Personal Information Collection And Use

We do not collect personal data in any way. The only data of yours that is transferred anywhere is the data used to store the content generated by the app (lists, settings, etc.) that is backed up to your personal iCloud account, if applicable. This data is not accessible by or shared with us and only you can access it.

Anonymized analytics relating to crashes and performance metrics may be shared if the setting "Share With App Developers" is enabled in the iOS Settings app. This data contains no personal information and is simply used to resolve crashing or performance issues within our app, if they are present. To opt out of the collection of this data, simply disable the "Share With App Developers" setting in your iOS device's Settings app.
