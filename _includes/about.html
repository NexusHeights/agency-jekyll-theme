About

A brief history of the company and what we've done.

  • December 2015

    Nexus Heights Founded

    Nexus Heights was founded in Rogersville, TN. It set out to make the best apps it could, and to try to make an impact in the mobile space.

  • March 2016

    First iOS app released

    Nexus Heights first iOS app, RNG Master, officially goes live in the App Store!

  • March 2016

    First Apple TV app released

    RNG Master TV, a port of RNG Master, is released! It marks the first time Nexus Heights releases an app on Apple TV.

  • February 2017

    First MacOS app released

    Image Resizifier, the first MacOS app by Nexus Heights, goes live. It is a developer tool for resizing image for use in Apples Xcode.
